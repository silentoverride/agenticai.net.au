## 1. What does the $1,200 AUD assessment include?

The AI Business Assessment is a structured 20–30 minute discovery call followed by a comprehensive, tailored report. Here's what's included:

- **Discovery interview** with our AI intake specialist, Annie, covering your workflows, tools, pain points, and automation opportunities
- **Workflow audit** — we map where your team loses time to manual, repeated tasks

- **Financial impact analysis** — estimated hours saved per week, annual value, and tool cost comparison
- **RevealDeck report** — a polished, shareable slide deck (PDF-exportable) that you can present to your team, board, or investors
- **Portal access** — your report lives in your private client portal at [agenticai.net.au/portal](https://agenticai.net.au/portal), accessible anytime
- **30-minute follow-up consultation** — book a complimentary session with our team to discuss implementation, prioritise your roadmap, and get answers to technical questions

The $1,200 is a one-time fee. No subscriptions, no hidden costs, no lock-in.

## 2. How long does the report take?

The report is typically ready within **48 hours** of payment confirmation.

Here's the timeline:
1. **Call completes** — Annie conducts the 20–30 minute intake (same day you book)
2. **Payment confirmed** — Stripe processes your payment securely
3. **Analysis pipeline runs** — our system extracts structured data from the call, researches tools, runs LLM analysis, and generates your RevealDeck report (usually 30–60 minutes, queued automatically)
4. **Quality check** — a human reviews the report for accuracy and completeness
5. **Delivered to portal** — you receive an email notification with a link to your report
6. **Follow-up booked** — at your convenience, book the complimentary 30-minute consultation

During busy periods, this may extend to 72 hours. If you're on a tight deadline, mention it during the call and we'll do our best to prioritise.

## 3. What happens after the assessment?

After the assessment, you have four paths:

### Path A: Self-implementation (most common)
Use the RevealDeck report and implementation roadmap to roll out the recommendations yourself or with your existing team. The report includes tool names, pricing, setup steps, and links to documentation. Approximately 60% of clients choose this path.

### Path B: Done-with-you implementation
Work alongside our team to implement the roadmap. We provide guided setup, configuration support, integration assistance, and training for your staff. Pricing is scoped after the assessment based on complexity.

### Path C: Done-for-you implementation
Our team implements everything end-to-end — tool selection, setup, integration, data migration, staff training, and handover. This is typically for businesses with no internal technical capacity.

### Path D: Start with quick wins only
Implement the 4-day quick wins outlined in the report first, then return later for deeper phases when you're ready. This lowers upfront cost and risk.

There's no pressure or obligation to proceed with any implementation work. The report is yours to keep regardless.

## 5. Do you implement the recommendations?

Yes — implementation is available, but it's **not included** in the $1,200 assessment fee. Think of the assessment as a paid discovery phase that gives you a clear action plan. Implementation is a separate engagement.

Here's how implementation works:

1. **Review the report together** — In your complimentary 30-minute follow-up, we walk through the roadmap, answer questions, and scope the work
2. **Receive a proposal** — We provide a fixed-price or retainer proposal based on your roadmap, team size, and technical complexity

4. **Handover and training** — Once implemented, we train your team and document everything

Typical implementation timelines:
- **Quick wins only**: 1–2 weeks
- **Phase 1 (core automations)**: 4–6 weeks
- **Full roadmap**: 3–6 months

We're an Australian-based consultancy with experience across trades, professional services, e-commerce, SaaS, and healthcare. All work is quoted upfront — no time-and-materials surprises.

## 7. Is my data safe? What about privacy?

Yes — data security and privacy are built into every layer of the assessment.

### Call data
- Call recordings and transcripts are stored in **Cloudflare's Australian data centres** (Sydney region)
- We do not sell, share, or train AI models on your data
- Personal identifiers (names, emails, phone numbers) are stored only in your private portal and our secure database
- Call recordings are retained for quality review but can be deleted on request

### Payment data
- All payments are processed by **Stripe**, a PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant provider. We never see or store your card details.
- Stripe receipts are accessible in your portal

### Portal access
- Your portal is protected by **Clerk authentication** (bank-grade identity verification)
- We use 2-factor authentication for admin access
- Reports are only accessible to authenticated users with your email address

### Compliance
- We comply with **Australian Privacy Principles** (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988
- If your business is subject to additional regulations (healthcare: AHPRA/NDIS, financial services: RG 274), let us know during the call and we'll note compliance constraints in your report

### Data deletion
- To request deletion of your data, email [hello@agenticai.net.au](mailto:hello@agenticai.net.au) with subject "Data Deletion Request"
- We will delete all call recordings, transcripts, reports, and personal data within 7 business days
- Receipts and payment records are retained for 7 years as required by Australian tax law

### After the call
If the call has been completed and the report has been generated, the assessment fee is **non-refundable**. This is because:
- The call consumes Retell API credits and LLM processing time
- The pipeline runs immediately upon payment, generating a report tailored to your business
- Human quality review is performed before delivery

However, if the report is materially incomplete, inaccurate, or fails to address the scope discussed in the call, we will:

- Re-schedule the call at no cost
- Re-run the pipeline

## 9. How is this different from hiring a consultant?

The AI Business Assessment is faster, cheaper, and more structured than a traditional consulting engagement. Here's the comparison:

| | **Traditional Consultant** | **Agentic AI Assessment** |
|---|---------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Time to deliverable** | 2–4 weeks of back-and-forth | 48 hours from call to report |
| **Cost** | $5,000–$15,000+ | $1,200 flat fee |
| **Discovery process** | Multiple meetings, surveys, emails | Single 20–30 minute structured call |
| **Deliverable format** | Word doc or PowerPoint (often generic) | Interactive RevealDeck report with charts, roadmap, and tool cards |
| **Tool research** | Manual; consultant's personal knowledge | Automated research across 200+ tools + Perplexity AI for current pricing |
| **Implementation** | Usually requires a separate proposal | Roadmap included; implementation optional and scoped post-report |

**When to choose a traditional consultant:**
- You need deep organisational change management
- You're rearchitecting your entire business model

**When to choose the AI Assessment:**
- You know you need automation but don't know where to start
- You want a clear, ranked action plan with specific tools
- You need to present a business case to stakeholders quickly
- You want to test one phase before committing to a larger engagement

Many clients use our assessment as the first step, then engage us or a traditional consultant for deeper implementation.

## 10. What if Annie doesn't understand my industry?

Annie is trained on a cross-industry knowledge base covering trades, professional services, e-commerce, SaaS, and healthcare. However, no AI is perfect — here's what happens if she encounters something unfamiliar:

1. **She'll ask clarifying questions** — "I'm not familiar with [specific tool]. Can you explain what it does in your workflow?"
2. **She'll note gaps** — If she can't adequately assess a domain, she flags it for human review
3. **Our team reviews every report** — Before delivery, a human checks for industry-specific accuracy and adds domain context if needed
4. **You'll get a follow-up call** — If significant gaps were found during the call, we reach out within 24 hours to fill them in

If your industry is highly specialised (e.g., nuclear engineering, aviation maintenance, international freight forwarding), mention this at the start of the call and we may recommend a **hybrid assessment**: Annie conducts the initial discovery, and a domain specialist follows up with a 15-minute deep-dive.
