## Executive Summary
Harbour Workflow Co is losing around six hours a week to repetitive manual admin, including copying email enquiries into a spreadsheet and sending follow-ups by hand, which has also led to poor proposal visibility. The owner is ready for a phased approach, beginning with a simple CRM and automated reminders before exploring more advanced AI agents.

## Pain Points
- **Manual email-to-spreadsheet data entry** (high, daily): New enquiries are manually copied from email into a spreadsheet every morning, creating a repetitive, error-prone start to the day.
- **Hand-sent follow-up reminders** (medium, daily): Follow-up reminders are sent manually, increasing the risk of missed touchpoints and consuming valuable consulting time.
- **Lost proposal status visibility** (high, weekly): Without a centralised pipeline, the team loses track of where each proposal stands, making forecasting and prioritisation difficult.
- **High weekly admin overhead** (medium, weekly): The combination of copying data, manual reminders, and spreadsheet management consumes approximately six hours per week.

## Quick Wins
- **Deploy a simple CRM with email capture** — Connect the enquiry inbox to a lightweight CRM to auto-create contact and deal records, eliminating the morning spreadsheet routine.
  - Effort: low, Impact: high, ~3 hrs/week saved
- **Automate follow-up reminder workflows** — Use the CRM's built-in automation to trigger reminder emails or tasks based on deal stage or last-contact date, removing manual follow-up work.
  - Effort: low, Impact: medium, ~2 hrs/week saved

## Deeper Opportunities
- **AI email parsing agent** — Automatically extract key enquiry details from incoming emails and populate CRM fields before human review, further reducing manual data entry.
  - Setup cost: $2500 AUD, Monthly value: $1000 AUD
- **AI proposal drafting assistant** — Generate first-draft proposals using CRM deal data and historical templates to accelerate turnaround times and improve win rates.
  - Setup cost: $3500 AUD, Monthly value: $1500 AUD
- **Advanced pipeline analytics and reporting** — Implement automated dashboards to track proposal win rates, cycle times, and follow-up effectiveness for continuous process optimisation.
  - Setup cost: $1500 AUD, Monthly value: $800 AUD

## Recommended Tools
- **HubSpot CRM** (CRM) — Centralise enquiries, replace the spreadsheet, track proposal status, and automate follow-up reminders.
  - ~$45/month | Setup: low
- **Zapier** (Automation) — Parse and route enquiry emails into the CRM if native inbox integration requires additional logic or formatting.
  - ~$30/month | Setup: low

## Implementation Roadmap
### Foundation (1-2)
- Select and configure a lightweight CRM (e.g., HubSpot)
- Connect the enquiry email inbox to auto-create records
- Import existing spreadsheet data and map fields
- Define proposal pipeline stages for clear status tracking

### Automation (3-4)
- Build automated follow-up reminder workflows by deal stage
- Set up deal ownership and task notifications
- Train the team on the new pipeline process
- Retire the manual spreadsheet and hand-sent reminders

### AI Exploration (5-8)
- Evaluate email parsing and AI drafting tools
- Pilot one AI agent (e.g., proposal assistant or email parser)
- Measure time savings and pipeline visibility improvements
- Plan broader rollout based on pilot results
