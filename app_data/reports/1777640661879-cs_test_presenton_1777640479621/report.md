## Executive Summary
Deck Verification Services currently relies on manual processes for enquiry handling, quote tracking in spreadsheets, and reminder follow-ups, creating significant administrative overhead. Implementing a CRM with automated workflows and AI-driven intake will streamline operations and reduce manual data entry.

## Pain Points
- **Manual Enquiry Processing** (high, daily): All incoming enquiries are processed by hand with no automated routing or capture system.
- **Spreadsheet-Based Quote Tracking** (high, daily): Quote requests are manually copied into spreadsheets, increasing risk of errors and consuming admin time.
- **Manual Reminder & Follow-Up** (medium, daily): Reminders and follow-ups are sent manually, leading to inconsistent client communication and potential missed opportunities.
- **No Centralised CRM Workflow** (high, daily): Absence of a CRM means no structured pipeline or automated workflow for managing leads from intake to quote.

## Quick Wins
- **Deploy CRM with Automated Follow-Up** — Implement an off-the-shelf CRM to capture enquiries and trigger automated email follow-up sequences.
  - Effort: low, Impact: high, ~4 hrs/week saved
- **Replace Manual Data Entry with Web Forms** — Use online forms that feed enquiry data directly into the CRM or a structured spreadsheet, eliminating manual copying.
  - Effort: low, Impact: medium, ~3 hrs/week saved
- **Automated Reminder Sequences** — Configure email templates and automated reminder workflows within the CRM to replace hand-sent reminders.
  - Effort: low, Impact: medium, ~2 hrs/week saved

## Deeper Opportunities
- **AI-Powered Intake Agent** — Deploy an AI chatbot or voice agent to qualify initial enquiries, collect verification details, and book appointments before human review.
  - Setup cost: $3000 AUD, Monthly value: $2000 AUD
- **End-to-End Quote Workflow Automation** — Connect enquiry sources, CRM, and proposal tools to auto-generate quote requests and update pipeline stages without manual intervention.
  - Setup cost: $2500 AUD, Monthly value: $1500 AUD
- **Centralised Knowledge System** — Build a searchable knowledge base for verification standards, pricing guidelines, and onboarding materials to reduce repetitive internal queries.
  - Setup cost: $1500 AUD, Monthly value: $800 AUD

## Recommended Tools
- **HubSpot CRM** (CRM) — Centralise contacts, manage enquiry pipeline, and automate follow-up emails
  - ~$70/month | Setup: low
- **Zapier** (Automation) — Integrate enquiry forms, spreadsheets, and CRM to remove manual data copying
  - ~$35/month | Setup: low
- **Typeform** (Forms) — Capture structured enquiry and quote request details from the website or email links
  - ~$45/month | Setup: low
- **Tidio** (AI Chatbot) — Provide initial AI-driven enquiry intake and qualification on the website
  - ~$70/month | Setup: medium

## Implementation Roadmap
### Foundation & Capture (1-2)
- Audit current enquiry sources and spreadsheet structure
- Configure HubSpot CRM with custom pipeline stages for verification services
- Deploy web forms for quote requests and link to CRM
- Import existing spreadsheet data into CRM

### Automation & Follow-Up (3-4)
- Build automated follow-up email sequences for new enquiries
- Set up reminder workflows for open quotes and pending responses
- Connect forms to CRM via Zapier to eliminate manual copying
- Train team on new CRM workflow and contact management

### AI Intake & Optimisation (5-8)
- Deploy AI chatbot for initial intake and qualification
- Create knowledge base with verification guidelines and pricing
- Review automation performance and refine messaging
- Document standard operating procedures for the new workflow
