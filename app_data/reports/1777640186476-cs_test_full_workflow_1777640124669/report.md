## Executive Summary
Lorie's local service business relies entirely on manual processes, with enquiries tracked in spreadsheets and follow-ups handled by hand, leading to lost leads and poor visibility. She wants to establish a simple CRM workflow and automated follow-up before exploring more advanced solutions like AI phone agents. The immediate priority is replacing spreadsheet tracking with a centralized system that captures both phone and email enquiries.

## Pain Points
- **Manual spreadsheet data entry** (high, daily): All customer enquiry details are entered by hand into spreadsheets, creating delays and risk of human error.
- **Manual follow-up execution** (medium, daily): Follow-up messages for quotes and reminders are sent manually, consuming significant time and leading to inconsistent communication.
- **Lost customer tracking and missed quotes** (high, daily): Without a unified workflow, the team frequently loses track of which customers require quotes or reminders, resulting in missed revenue opportunities.

## Quick Wins
- **Deploy a simple CRM with intake forms** — Replace spreadsheets with a free CRM that captures email enquiries automatically via forms and provides a central dashboard for quote and reminder tracking.
  - Effort: low, Impact: high, ~3 hrs/week saved
- **Automate email follow-up sequences** — Set up automated email workflows within the CRM to send quote follow-ups and reminders based on deal stage or last contact date.
  - Effort: low, Impact: high, ~2 hrs/week saved

## Deeper Opportunities
- **AI phone agent for enquiry intake** — Once the CRM workflow is mature, implement an AI phone agent to handle initial enquiries, qualify leads, and log details directly into the CRM.
  - Setup cost: $5000 AUD, Monthly value: $2000 AUD
- **End-to-end intake automation** — Integrate phone, email, and web forms into a fully automated pipeline that routes enquiries, schedules reminders, and updates deal stages without manual input.
  - Setup cost: $3000 AUD, Monthly value: $1500 AUD

## Recommended Tools
- **HubSpot CRM** (CRM) — Centralize customer data, track quotes, and automate follow-up emails and reminders
  - ~$0/month | Setup: low
- **Zapier** (Automation) — Connect email inbox and web forms to the CRM to trigger automated workflows and reduce manual data entry
  - ~$40/month | Setup: low

## Implementation Roadmap
### Foundation (1-2)
- Sign up for HubSpot CRM and import existing spreadsheet data
- Create intake forms for web and email enquiries
- Set up basic deal stages for quotes and reminders

### Automation (3-4)
- Configure automated email follow-up sequences for quote reminders
- Connect email inbox via Zapier to log enquiries automatically
- Train team on new CRM workflow and dashboard

### Optimisation (5-8)
- Review follow-up performance and adjust messaging
- Identify remaining manual phone intake gaps
- Document requirements and budget for future AI phone agent
