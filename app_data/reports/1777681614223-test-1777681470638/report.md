## Executive Summary
Test Marketing Agency's owner loses 10+ hours weekly to manual reporting, email sorting, lost meeting actions, and design bottlenecks. The owner explicitly prioritises automated client dashboards as the highest-impact fix, which would free strategic time currently consumed by $25/hour compilation work.

## Pain Points
- **Manual Client Reporting** (high, weekly): Every Monday the owner spends 3-4 hours pulling data from Google Analytics, Meta Ads, and the scheduling tool into spreadsheets for clients. The work is described as 'soul-crushing' and causes the owner to fall behind.
- **Lost Meeting Notes & Action Items** (high, daily): Daily standups and client check-ins lack proper notes. Action items are forgotten, and the owner must chase staff on Slack to confirm what was agreed.
- **Email Overload & Noise** (high, daily): The owner is CC'd on everything and receives roughly 200 emails a day, mostly noise or sales outreach. This consumes about 2 hours of sorting time daily.
- **Inefficient Design Workflow** (medium, weekly): The senior designer spends 6 hours a week on social media graphics and presentation decks that junior staff should handle. Junior staff lack templates and brand guidelines, so all work routes back to the senior designer.

## Quick Wins
- **Auto-Pull Client Reporting Dashboards** — Use no-code automation to connect Google Analytics, Meta Ads, and the scheduling tool so numbers populate automatically for review instead of manual compilation every Monday.
  - Effort: low, Impact: high, ~3.5 hrs/week saved
- **AI Meeting Notes & Action Item Tracking** — Deploy an AI meeting assistant to transcribe daily standups and client check-ins, extract action items, and push them to Slack so nothing is lost and the owner stops chasing staff.
  - Effort: low, Impact: medium, ~2 hrs/week saved
- **AI Email Filtering & Prioritisation** — Apply an AI-powered email sorter to the owner's high-volume inbox to filter noise and sales outreach, cutting the 2 hours/day currently spent sorting CC'd emails.
  - Effort: low, Impact: high, ~5 hrs/week saved
- **Canva Brand Kits for Junior Designers** — Create standardised templates and brand kits so junior staff can produce social graphics and presentation decks without sending everything back to the senior designer.
  - Effort: low, Impact: medium, ~3 hrs/week saved

## Deeper Opportunities
- **Client Self-Service Portal** — Build the client portal the owner has wanted for two years, giving clients direct access to automated dashboards and reducing repetitive status requests.
  - Setup cost: $8000 AUD, Monthly value: $3000 AUD
- **Integrated Agency Operations Layer** — Connect reporting, meeting actions, design requests, and email into a single automated workflow so data flows across tools without manual handoffs.
  - Setup cost: $5000 AUD, Monthly value: $2000 AUD
- **AI-Assisted Business Development** — Use the freed owner hours for strategy and new business by deploying an AI agent to qualify leads, draft proposals, and manage initial outreach.
  - Setup cost: $4000 AUD, Monthly value: $1500 AUD

## Recommended Tools
- **Zapier** (Manual Reporting) — Automate tasks by connecting Google Analytics, Meta Ads, and the scheduling tool to eliminate manual data pulling into spreadsheets.
  - ~$30/month | Setup: low
- **Fireflies.ai** (Poor Meeting Notes) — AI meeting assistant that transcribes standups and check-ins, extracts action items, and integrates with Slack to prevent lost tasks.
  - ~$25/month | Setup: low
- **SaneBox** (Email Overload) — AI-powered email sorter that filters noise and sales outreach, reducing inbox overload from 200 emails a day.
  - ~$15/month | Setup: low
- **Canva Magic Studio** (Inefficient Design Workflow) — AI design platform with templates and brand kits enabling juniors to create social graphics and decks without senior oversight.
  - ~$20/month | Setup: low
- **Make** (Manual Reporting) — Streamline workflows with intuitive visual automation across numerous apps and processes to handle reporting from multiple data sources.
  - ~$20/month | Setup: medium

## Implementation Roadmap
### Foundation: Reporting & Email (1-2)
- Connect Google Analytics, Meta Ads and scheduling tool via Zapier to auto-populate client dashboard or spreadsheet
- Deploy SaneBox to filter noise and sales outreach from the owner's 200 daily emails
- Validate automated report accuracy against previous manual Monday reports

### Operations: Meetings & Design (3-4)
- Roll out Fireflies.ai for daily standups and client check-ins with Slack integration for action items
- Build Canva brand kit and social media templates so junior staff can self-serve design requests
- Document basic design guidelines to reduce senior designer review time

### Scale: Integration & Portal (5-8)
- Integrate meeting action items into project management workflow
- Scope and prototype client portal for automated reporting access
- Review time savings and reallocate owner hours to strategy and business development
