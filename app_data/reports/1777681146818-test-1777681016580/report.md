## Executive Summary
The owner of this 8-person digital marketing agency is trapped doing $25/hour manual work, losing 10+ hours weekly to client reporting, email triage, and follow-up chasing. Implementing off-the-shelf automation for reporting, email, and meeting notes can immediately reclaim strategic time, while deeper investments in a client portal and brand knowledge system will unlock senior designer capacity and improve client experience.

## Pain Points
- **Manual Client Reporting** (high, weekly): Every Monday the owner spends 3-4 hours soul-crushingly pulling data from Google Analytics, Meta Ads, and the scheduling tool into spreadsheets for clients, causing them to always be behind.
- **Lost Meeting Action Items** (medium, daily): Daily standups and client check-ins lack proper notes, so action items get lost, staff forget commitments, and the owner ends up chasing people on Slack.
- **Email Overload** (high, daily): The owner is CC'd on everything and receives roughly 200 emails a day, most of which are noise or sales outreach, requiring about 2 hours daily just to sort.
- **Senior Designer Bottleneck** (medium, weekly): The senior designer loses 6 hours a week creating social media graphics and presentation decks that junior staff should handle, but juniors lack templates or brand guidelines so everything routes back to her.

## Quick Wins
- **Auto-Pull Reporting Dashboards** — Connect Google Analytics, Meta Ads, and the scheduling tool via Zapier, Make, or n8n to automatically populate a live dashboard or spreadsheet, eliminating the Monday manual compilation ritual.
  - Effort: low, Impact: high, ~3.5 hrs/week saved
- **AI Email Triage** — Deploy SaneBox or Superhuman to automatically filter sales outreach and noise, surfacing only important client and team emails so the owner stops spending 2 hours a day sorting.
  - Effort: low, Impact: high, ~4 hrs/week saved
- **Automated Meeting Notes & Action Items** — Implement Fireflies.ai or ClickUp AI to transcribe standups and client check-ins, auto-extract action items, and end the manual follow-up chasing on Slack.
  - Effort: low, Impact: medium, ~2.5 hrs/week saved
- **Self-Service Design Templates** — Create Canva brand kits, social media templates, and deck frameworks so junior staff can produce client-ready assets without routing everything through the senior designer.
  - Effort: low, Impact: medium, ~6 hrs/week saved

## Deeper Opportunities
- **Client Portal Development** — Build a self-service client portal for dashboards, reports, and project updates. The owner has wanted this for two years but never had time to execute because of manual reporting work.
  - Setup cost: $8000 AUD, Monthly value: $2000 AUD
- **AI Narrative Reporting Agent** — Layer AI on top of automated dashboards to generate written insights, trends, and client recommendations, moving the agency from raw data delivery to strategic advisory.
  - Setup cost: $5000 AUD, Monthly value: $1500 AUD
- **Brand Operations Playbook** — Formalize brand guidelines, design decision trees, and asset libraries into a central knowledge system to fully eliminate the senior designer bottleneck and onboard juniors faster.
  - Setup cost: $3000 AUD, Monthly value: $1000 AUD

## Recommended Tools
- **Zapier** (Manual Reporting) — Automate the weekly client reporting workflow by connecting Google Analytics, Meta Ads, and the scheduling tool to eliminate manual spreadsheet compilation.
  - ~$30/month | Setup: low
- **SaneBox** (Email Overload) — Filter noise and sales outreach from the owner's 200+ daily emails, prioritizing important client and team messages.
  - ~$7/month | Setup: low
- **Fireflies.ai** (Poor Meeting Notes) — Auto-transcribe daily standups and client check-ins and extract action items to prevent lost follow-ups and Slack chasing.
  - ~$15/month | Setup: low
- **Canva Magic Studio** (Inefficient Design Workflow) — Empower junior staff to create social graphics and presentation decks via AI templates and brand kits, reducing senior designer bottleneck.
  - ~$20/month | Setup: low
- **Make** (Manual Reporting) — Visual automation alternative to pull and process data from multiple marketing sources into unified client reports.
  - ~$20/month | Setup: medium

## Implementation Roadmap
### Foundation (1-2)
- Connect Google Analytics, Meta Ads, and scheduling tool to Zapier/Make/n8n to auto-populate reporting dashboard or spreadsheet
- Install SaneBox on owner inbox and configure folders to filter sales outreach and low-priority noise
- Deploy Fireflies.ai or ClickUp AI for all standups and client check-ins to capture action items automatically

### Workflow Unlock (3-4)
- Build Canva brand kit, social templates, and deck frameworks for junior staff self-service
- Train team on new design workflow and document brand guidelines
- Review automated report outputs and refine data mappings based on client feedback

### Scale & Strategic Build (5-8)
- Scope requirements and begin build of the client portal the owner has wanted for two years
- Evaluate AI narrative layer on top of reporting dashboards for strategic client insights
- Optimize email rules and meeting note integrations based on team usage data
