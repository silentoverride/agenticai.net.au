# AI Business Assessment Business Plan

Source material from an interview with Corey Ganim (Return My Time / Build With AI Podcast / audittemplate.ai) on The Koerner Office Podcast. This is the foundational blueprint for the Agentic AI service model.

## Reliability

**High.** This is a primary-source interview with the founder of the assessment model Agentic AI is implementing. Corey describes his actual business evolution, pricing, client acquisition methods, and operational details. The document includes specific numbers, tool names, case studies, and a 30-day launch plan.

## Key Claims

1. **Market size:** AI consulting market $11B → $117B by 2035 at 26% CAGR
2. **SMB gap:** 99/100 small business owners need this service; 72% of companies use AI in some function but most SMBs don't know where to start
3. **Service evolution:** Four iterations — physical shadowing → Loom → Zoom → AI voice agent (Annie)
4. **Pricing anchor:** $1,000 assessment fee creates psychological buy-in for $3,500–$10,000 upsells
5. **Four upsell categories:** Process optimization ($3–5K), Automation builds ($1–3K), Knowledge systems ($2–5K + recurring), Speed-to-lead agents ($2–5K)
6. **Seven client acquisition methods:** Local AI meetup, door-knocking, free assessments for network, AI office hours, LinkedIn content, referral partnerships, DM outreach
7. **Adjacent revenue streams:** Workshops, certification/training, white-label implementation, retainer partnerships, vertical-specific products
8. **Time to start:** 7 days of learning AI tools is sufficient to begin selling assessments

## Concepts Covered

- Service model and pricing psychology
- Voice agent interview process (Annie on Retell)
- Report generation pipeline (Kimi K2.6 via Ollama Cloud + Presenton)
- Upsell menu and specialization strategy
- Client acquisition playbook
- 30-day launch plan for new consultants
- 6-month revenue projections
- Real case studies (wedding venue, business brokerage, barber school, realtor office, mortgage broker)
