# Illustrations

Download free SVG illustrations from unDraw and save them here with these exact filenames.

| Filename | unDraw Search Term | Section |
|----------|-------------------|---------|
| `undraw-conversation.svg` | "conversation" | From intake call to action plan |
| `undraw-services.svg` | "features" or "services" | Services list |
| `undraw-analytics.svg` | "analytics" or "data" | AI processes the findings |
| `undraw-report.svg` | "report" or "documents" | What you receive after the assessment |
| `undraw-building.svg` | "building" or "constructor" | Common builds that follow the report |
| `undraw-launch.svg` | "launching" or "rocket" | Ready to see where AI can save your business time? |

## Steps

1. Go to [https://undraw.co/illustrations](https://undraw.co/illustrations)
2. Search for the term
3. Pick a color that matches your brand
4. Download as SVG
5. Save in this folder with the filename from the table above

All illustrations are open-source and free for commercial use.

## Custom color tip

Change the fill color in the SVG to match your brand. The default unDraw color is `#6C63FF`.
